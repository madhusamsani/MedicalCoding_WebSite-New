Our Commitment to Compliance

Protecting Patient Health Information (PHI) is at the Core of Everything We Do. We Operate Under Strict HIPAA Guidelines and Align with Industry Best Practices to Ensure Confidentiality, Integrity, and Availability of Sensitive Data.

Administrative Safeguards

  • Comprehensive HIPAA Policies and Mandatory Staff Training & Training
  • Minimum Necessary Access Enforcement to Reduce Exposure Risks
  • Documented Incident Response and Breach Management Procedures

Technical Safeguards

  • VPN-Only Access with Enforced Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)
  • Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) with Real-Time Audit Logs
  • Encryption in Transit and at Rest (Platform-Dependent) to Protect PHI Integrity

Physical Safeguards

  • Secure Work areas with Restricted Access for Authorized Personnel Only
  • Device Controls to Prevent Unauthorized Use or Data Extraction
  • Media Sanitization and Secure Backups to Safeguard Information Lifecycle Management

This marketing site does not collect PHI. Operational safeguards apply within contracted environments.
